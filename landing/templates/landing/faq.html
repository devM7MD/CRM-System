Find answers to the most common questions about our services, platform, and how we can help grow your e-commerce business.

What is Atlas services?

Atlas services is an all-in-one digital platform that provides end-to-end e-commerce solutions. We handle everything from inventory sourcing, order management, fulfillment, delivery tracking, to payment processing - allowing you to focus on growing your business while we take care of operations.

How can Atlas services help my e-commerce business?

We provide a comprehensive solution that streamlines your entire e-commerce operation. By integrating with your online store, we handle inventory sourcing, warehouse management, order processing, confirmation calls, fulfillment, and payment collection. This allows you to scale your business without the operational headaches. Our platform provides full visibility of the entire process, helping you make data-driven decisions for growth.

How does the payment collection work?

We provide Cash on Delivery (COD) collection services across various regions. Our delivery agents collect payment from customers at the time of delivery and the funds are securely processed and remitted to your merchant account. We handle all financial reconciliation and provide detailed reporting through our platform.

How quickly do I receive payments from COD orders?

We process and remit payments on a weekly basis. Once payments are collected, they go through reconciliation and are transferred to your merchant account according to the payment schedule in your contract. You can track all transactions in real-time through our merchant dashboard.

What countries do you deliver to?

We currently operate in 9 countries across the MENA region and are constantly expanding our reach. Our primary markets include Saudi Arabia, UAE, Egypt, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar, Jordan, and Lebanon. Check our coverage map on the platform for the most up-to-date information on delivery areas.

How long does delivery typically take?

Delivery times vary by location, but we typically deliver within 1-3 business days for major cities and 3-5 business days for other areas. Express delivery options are available in select locations. You can track shipments in real-time through our platform and provide this tracking information to your customers.

Which e-commerce platforms do you integrate with?

We offer seamless integration with major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, and custom-built stores. Our API-based integration allows for real-time order syncing, inventory updates, and delivery status sharing. For platforms not directly supported, we offer alternative solutions like CSV imports or custom integration development.

How do I get started with Atlas services?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free trial on our website, and one of our onboarding specialists will contact you to set up your account. The onboarding process typically takes 2-3 business days, which includes platform integration, account setup, and training. We provide full support throughout the process to ensure you're up and running quickly.
