Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new sourcing request?

Navigate to the Sourcing section and click on "New Request" button. Fill in the required information about the product you need sourced and submit the form.

How can I track my orders?

Go to the Orders section where you can view all your orders. Click on any order to see detailed information including tracking and delivery status.

How do I manage my inventory?

The Inventory section provides tools to track stock levels, set up alerts for low inventory, and manage warehouse locations. Use the filters to find specific products quickly.

How can I update my account information?

Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Your Profile". From there, you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage notification preferences.
